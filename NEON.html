360° LED Neon

Adura’s 360° LED Neon offers seamless all-around illumination with a flexible design that can be bent freely to achieve a variety of shapes and lighting effects. Compatible with a wide range of mounting accessories, it’s ideal for creating artistic and atmospheric lighting in office spaces, galleries, cafes, and more. Optional dust-free matte finishes provide a clean visual appearance. The LEDs feature a long lifetime of over 50,000 hours and ensure color consistency with SDCM ≤ 3 (1 bin).

Pixel Neon LED Strips

Adura’s Pixel Neon LEDs combine digital control with vibrant RGB and RGBW color effects. Controlled by either SPI or DMX512 protocols, these LED strips enable dynamic lighting features like color gradients, chasing, pulsing, and animations. An integrated accessory system ensures fast, stable setup and synchronization for vivid visual displays.
